Common Questions

Understanding the MediMind Protocol

Is MediMind a doctor?

No. MediMind is an advanced health intelligence engine designed to provide informational support and triage. It should always be used as a supplement to, not a replacement for, professional medical consultation.

How is my data protected?

MediMind uses End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) and zero-data retention policies. Your clinical queries are decoupled from your identity, and all data processing occurs in highly secure, isolated neural environments.
